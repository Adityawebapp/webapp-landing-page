Process We Follow

Strategy

It is this phase of our digital process that is probably the most critical to your project. During this phase, we gain a deep understanding of your organisation, vision, and goals. Our team analyses your requirements to determine the functionalities that must be included in the customised software.

Creative

Designing a website or an app to meet your goals starts at the creative phase, translating the brand identity into online creations. We create a conceptual design of what the mobile app or website will look like.

Design

After defining your brand identity, ideas, and values, we use artistic inspiration to create the design of your bespoke software. This step is highly collaborative and interactive. We will be in constant communication with you to revise the strategy based on your ideas and input. In addition, our developers use effective UI trends to design apps, games, and websites that are not only pleasing to the eye but also intuitive and consistent.

Content

Messaging and design must work together to communicate your brand identity, so it is only natural that these phases occur concurrently. After you complete the creative phase, you’ll have the design and message to serve as the basis for a functional website, game, or mobile app.

Development

As soon as your creative design is complete, our development team will create functional software as per your requirements. We have a team of experts in different technologies such as Android app development, iOS app development, PHP website development, Laravel website development, blockchain technologies, UI/UX designs, and many more to develop high-quality mobile apps, websites, and bespoke software for the different operating systems.

Testing

The zero-flaw goal we aim to reach is to build you a perfect website or app. Everything we design and build is put through a battery of tests to verify that all functions are implemented and that your website or app renders perfectly on all devices. Our developers carefully test every app, website, and game to ensure that they provide an excellent user experience and meet your requirements.

Launch

Data and content migrations are finalised, hosting is installed, and your website goes live on the production servers. We follow best practices when deploying apps on various app stores where they can be easily visible to prospective users. Once the project is live on the internet, we perform a final analysis to ensure that everything is running smoothly.

Evalution

After your website, app, or game is launched, we then test its functionality with live visitors. We use cutting-edge analytic tools to assess the site’s performance and visitor behaviours.
